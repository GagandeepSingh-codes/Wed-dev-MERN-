Units in CSS

Different types of units in CSS

  • Absolute Units:
    • px (pixels)
    • cm (centimeters)
    • mm (millimeters)
    • in (inches)
    • pt (points)
    • pc (picas)
  • Relative Units:
    • % (percentage)
    • em (relative to the font-size of the element)
    • rem (relative to the font-size of the root element)
    • vw (viewport width)
    • vh (viewport height)
    • vmin (minimum of viewport width and height)
    • vmax (maximum of viewport width and height)
